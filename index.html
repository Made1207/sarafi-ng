How it works

A single check before every disbursement

Sarafi sits alongside your existing underwriting process. It returns one thing: whether this applicant has active loans or defaults recorded at other lenders in the network, right now.

01

Integrate once

One REST endpoint. One API key. Full documentation available at api.sarafi.ng/docs. No proprietary SDK or special tooling required.

02

Send the BVN before disbursing

When an applicant submits, your system calls POST /v1/check with their BVN. The number is hashed immediately on receipt. Sarafi never stores it.

03

Receive a real-time signal

Sarafi checks the BVN against every active flag in the network and returns whether the applicant is flagged, how many flags exist, how many distinct lenders have flagged them, whether any flag is an active loan or a recorded default, and how many loans have been repaid on record. You make the final lending decision.

04

Contribute to the network

Report defaulters via POST /v1/report. Every lender who contributes improves the signal for every other member. Value compounds with scale.

Why Sarafi

The signal bureaus do not provide

Bureau checks cover formal bank lending. SMS scoring covers your own app's history. Neither tells you whether this applicant opened three other loan applications in the past two weeks. Sarafi answers that question.

Real-time cross-lender exposure

Check whether an applicant has active loans or defaults at other network lenders at the moment they apply — not days later when a bureau refreshes.

Neutral infrastructure

No lender sees another's data. Sarafi is the trusted middle layer. Competing lenders share data with Sarafi, not with each other.

A network that compounds

Every new lender who joins improves the signal for every existing member. Early partners accumulate a data advantage that grows over time.

Complements your existing stack

Sarafi does not replace your bureau check or SMS scoring. It adds the one signal they cannot supply: current exposure across other apps.

Built for regulatory scrutiny

Every check and every report is logged immutably. An audit trail that holds up to compliance review, from the first day of operation.

Minimal engineering lift

A standard REST API with full documentation. The integration surface is a single endpoint with a straightforward JSON request and response.

Privacy

Your portfolio is never visible to competitors

The reason lenders share data with Sarafi rather than directly with each other is structural. The system is designed so that sharing reveals nothing a competitor could act on.

  • BVNs are SHA-256 hashed with a secret key on receipt. Raw numbers never enter the database.
  • Querying lenders receive only a signal — never the identity of lenders who submitted flags.
  • Reason notes submitted with a flag are never returned through the API.
  • Lenders can only resolve flags they themselves submitted.
  • Every API call is logged immutably for accountability and compliance.
